Biography

ANDHRAY is a visionary industrial techno artist and producer who has been shaping the underground electronic music scene with her powerful, hypnotic soundscapes. As the founder of Industrial Girls Records, she champions experimental artists and pushes the boundaries of electronic music. Her performances are known for their raw energy and immersive atmosphere, captivating audiences across major festivals and clubs worldwide.
